# Mission: Becoming an AI Engineer

## Why
Numaan wants to become an **AI Engineer** — someone who builds and ships real,
LLM-powered applications (RAG systems, agents, APIs, production features). The
goal is practical capability: turning the AI models that already exist into
working products, not researching or inventing new ones.

## Success looks like
- Can build and deploy an LLM-powered app end-to-end (prompting → RAG →
  tool-using agent → API).
- Can choose and integrate the right model provider (Claude, OpenAI,
  open-source) and vector database for a given task.
- Can reason about a production AI system: evaluation, guardrails, cost,
  latency, observability.
- Can explain any concept on the roadmap clearly enough to defend it in an
  interview.

## Constraints
- ~2–3 hours available per day — can move at a good pace.
- Comfortable with *some* coding, but not an expert — keep code approachable.
- **Minimal appetite for math.** Teach math only as light intuition, never
  derivations. Skip depth.
- Learning follows `AI_Learning_Roadmap.md` (8 phases), but weighted toward the
  AI-Engineer destination (Phases 7–8).

## Out of scope
- Deep mathematics (proofs, manual backpropagation, matrix calculus).
- ML research / training large models from scratch.
- Data-science and data-engineering career tracks (touched only where they
  inform building).
