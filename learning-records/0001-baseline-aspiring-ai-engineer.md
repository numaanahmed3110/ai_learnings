# Baseline: aspiring AI Engineer, some coding, minimal math, 2–3 hrs/day

First session (2026-07-16) set the mission. Numaan's goal is to become an **AI
Engineer** who builds and ships LLM-powered applications — not a researcher or ML
engineer. Background is *"some coding"* (scripts / small apps, not expert).
Explicitly wants **minimal math** — intuition only, no depth. Has 2–3 hours/day.

Why this matters for future sessions: teach the roadmap weighted toward the
AI-Engineer destination (Phases 7–8: APIs, prompt engineering, RAG, agents,
vector DBs, production). Compress Phases 3–6 (math, ML theory, deep learning,
NLP) to conceptual literacy — enough to be conversant and to build, never
academic derivations. Low tolerance for math-heavy material; high tolerance for
hands-on building. See [[MISSION.md]].
